Step 2: How to sit

How to sit well

  • Sitting with good posture is dependent on many factors.
  • Position your computer screen level with your eyeline so that you don't need to tilt your head forward to look at it.
  • If using a laptop, a stand and separate keyboard and mouse can help to prevent you from hunching over forwards.
  • Sit your hips to the back of the chair and keep your pelvis neutral to avoid rounding the lower back.
  • Keep your shoulders relaxed and down.
