Step 3: Tips

Tips for Maintaining Good Posture

  • Take regular breaks - stand up and stretch every 30 minutes
  • Ensure your monitor is at eye level
  • Keep your feet flat on the floor
  • Invest in an ergonomic chair if possible
  • Keep your keyboard and mouse close to avoid reaching
  • Use PostureX consistently for the best results
